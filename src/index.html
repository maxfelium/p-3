El Camino de Santiago

What is it?

El Camino de Santiago is a series of ancient pilgrim's routes that begin all over the world and arrive in Santiago de Compostela. In ancient times, people would walk to the cathedral of Santiago to visit the tomb of the Apostole St. James. Nowadays, hundreds of thousands of people do the journey by foot but many don't do it for religious reasons. People embark on El Camino to spend time with themselves and reflect on their own life. Regardless of the reason to take this journey, this path has been taken on for thousands of years and everyone should walk it at least once.
